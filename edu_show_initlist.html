edu-show-initlist

Transform a std::initializer list

Default: Off

Examples:

#include <vector>
std::vector<int> vec{40, 2};

transforms into this:

#include <vector>
const int __list3_21[2]{40, 2};
std::vector<int, std::allocator<int> > vec = std::vector<int, std::allocator<int> >{std::initializer_list<int>{__list3_21, 2}, std::allocator<int>()};
